Refactoring portfolio webpage

Date: Jan 19, 2023.

Quite a few days back, as usual I was surfing on random accounts of github users and suddenly I landed upon webpage of Rohit Goswami, he is my colleague at LFortran, I haven't talked with him much often though and it brought up motivation in me to have a quick refactoring of my webpage.

So, !being highly motivate, I started searching for various templates, brrr who makes it from scratch. Don't take me wrong but "You will always find better design than yours ;)", so put efforts in searching those. I land upon this template, the design is quite simple and minimal. {The best part of this is that it is made using HTML5UP, which is a free and open source template.} The line in the bold braces is written by "Github Copilot" and it seems correct. Yeah so, the best part of this template is it provides me beautiful markdown, font style which makes me look a bit formal I can say. Overall it is a good template and I am happy with it.

Lastly, I would tell that I am not a good write, trying blog writting for very first time, but yeah one must surely refactor their personal webpage time to time it is an interesting task. All in all it was a great experience.
